✅ Resume Do’s & ❌ Don’ts

✅ Do’s

  • Keep it clear, concise, and ATS-friendly.
  • Use consistent font, spacing, and formatting.
  • Highlight relevant skills/projects.
  • Add action verbs.
  • Tailor resume for tech placements.
  • Include GitHub/Portfolio/LinkedIn links.
  • Proofread carefully.

❌ Don’ts

  • Don’t use fancy fonts, colors, or images.
  • Avoid long paragraphs — use bullet points.
  • Don’t include irrelevant details (age, full address, photo).
  • Don’t lie or exaggerate skills/projects.
  • Don’t clutter with too many skills.
  • Don’t use generic objectives.
  • Don’t forget to update it.
