Why PrepHub X?


PrepHub X is a placement preparation web hub created with the vision of simplifying the journey from coding to career. Many students struggle with scattered resources, unclear roadmaps, and lack of structured guidance during their placement journey. PrepHub X brings everything together curated DSA playlists, concise CS fundamentals notes, resume templates and tips, company-wise question lists, motivational content, and interview guidance into a single, easy-to-access platform. The goal of PrepHub X is to empower students with the right resources, clarity, and confidence to crack technical interviews and excel in campus placements. By combining technical knowledge, career skills, and motivation, this platform ensures that no student feels lost in their preparation journey.
