Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We're here to answer!
If you don't see your question here, drop us a line on our Contact page

What is PrepHub X? ×
PrepHub X is a placement preparation web hub that brings together all essential resources in one place. From DSA playlists and CS fundamentals notes to interview questions and resume guidance, it helps students prepare effectively without wasting time searching multiple platforms.
Is PrepHub X free to use? ×
Yes, all the resources, notes, and tools provided on PrepHub X are free to access.
What kind of DSA resources are available? ×
You’ll find curated playlists and a roadmap to cover important Data Structures and Algorithms for interviews.
Which CS fundamentals are covered? ×
PrepHub X includes concise notes and explanations for the four most important CS subjects asked in interviews: OOP, DBMS, Operating Systems, and Computer Networks. These are simplified to help you quickly revise before interviews.
Can I download notes from the website? ×
Yes, most notes and PDFs can be downloaded for offline use.
Does PrepHub X help with resume building? ×
Yes, we share resume templates and tips on do’s and don’ts to help you create an interview-ready resume.
Are there company-wise questions available? ×
Yes, there is a dedicated section with company-wise interview question lists. This helps students target their preparation for specific companies, especially those visiting their campus for placements.
What is the Motivational section? ×
The Motivational section is designed to keep students motivated during the stressful placement journey. It includes daily motivational quotes and selected interview experience videos that give insights into real-world interviews and help reduce anxiety.
What is the Coding Tip of the Day feature? ×
It’s a simple interactive tool that displays random coding tips to strengthen your problem-solving approach.
Does PrepHub X provide aptitude and puzzles? ×
Currently, PrepHub X focuses on DSA, CS fundamentals, and interview prep. Aptitude and puzzles may be added later.
Can I use PrepHub X for non-technical placements? ×
PrepHub X is mainly designed for technical and software-related placements. However, resume tips and motivational content can benefit all students.
Why should I use PrepHub X over other resources? ×
Most online resources are scattered across multiple websites, playlists, and blogs. PrepHub X organizes everything—DSA, core CS notes, resume templates, interview tips, and motivational content—into a single, easy-to-navigate platform. This saves time and makes preparation more focused.
